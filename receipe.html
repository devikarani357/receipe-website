Gulab jamun Receipe

Ingredients
  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Receipe Process

1.Heat oil or ghee in a deep pan over medium heat. Once hot, reduce the heat to low-medium.
2.Gently slide the prepared dough balls into the hot oil and fry until they turn golden brown.
3.Keep turning the balls for even frying.
4.Once fried, remove them from the oil and immediately place them into the warm sugar syrup.
5.Let them soak for at least 30 minutes to 1 hour, allowing the Gulab Jamuns to absorb the syrup and become soft.
6.Serve the Gulab Jamuns warm, garnished with chopped nuts if desired.
7.Enjoy your homemade Gulab Jamuns!
